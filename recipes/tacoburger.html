Taco Burger

This is the Taco Burger. A mix of two classic dishes which allows it to tastes out of this world.

The Ingredients are

  • One pound Ground Beef
  • One packet Taco Seasoning
  • Five slices Pepper Jack cheese
  • Five Hamburger Buns
  • A quarter cup sour cream
  • A quarter cup Salsa
  • One cup choppped fresh Cilantro
  • One Tomato, sliced
  • One avocado, sliced
  • Half onion, sliced

The Steps to take to complete the burger are as follows

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grates
  2. Mix Ground Beef and Taco Seasoning together with your hands. Split into five equal portions
  3. Cook the burgers for approximately five minutes and then flip adding Pepper Jack Cheese. Cook until desired Coloring
  4. Spread Sour Cream and Salsa on buns. Top with cooked patties, Cilantro, Tomato, Avocado, and Onion.
