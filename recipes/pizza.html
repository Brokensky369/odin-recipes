Double Bacon Ranch Pizza

Ingredients for Pizza

  • Four slices Bacon
  • Two Teaspoons Vegetable Oil
  • Ten Ounce container refrigerated Pizza Crust
  • One Teaspoon dry Ranch Seasoning, Divided
  • Third Cup Ranch Dressing
  • Six Ounces Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Third Cup Cubed, Cooked Chicken
  • Eigth Cup Sliced Red Onion
  • One Small Tomato, Sliced
  • One Tablespoon Butter
  1. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F
  2. Cook Bacon in large skillet flipping occasionally until browned. Remove and soak remaining grease off the Bacon
  3. Pour oil into twelve inch cast iron skillet. Spread evenly. Place dough in skillet and spread evenly forming a crust at the edge. Prick dough with fork all over
  4. Bake dough in oven for five minutes. Remove and reshape dough. Sprinkle two tablepoons of ranch dressing over top of dough. Spread ranch dressing on top.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella, chicken and red onion over pizza crust. Crumble bacon on top of the pizza.
  6. Bake in oven until cheese is melted and top is browned. Approximately Twenty minutes.
  7. Remove pizza from oven. Top with Tomato slices. Melt the butter and mix in ranch seasoning. Brush the edges of the crust for flavor.
  8. Set oven rack six inches from the top and turn to broiler setting
  9. Broil the pizza for two to three minutes to brown the cheese
